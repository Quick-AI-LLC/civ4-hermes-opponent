# Civ4 Tile Improvement Quick Reference (BFC Focus)

## Big Fat Cross (5x5 BFC)
- Center = city tile  
- 2 tiles cardinal, 1 diagonal each direction  
- Only these tiles can be improved early

## Improvement Priority (BFC)
1. **Food resources** – farms on corn, rice, wheat, flood plains  
2. **Production resources** – mines on hills, horses, iron, coal  
3. **Gold resources** – cottages on plains/grassland for long‑term gold  

## Tile Types & Yields (pre‑improvement)
- Plains/Grassland: 1 food, 1 production  
- Hills: +1 production (no food)  
- Forest: +1 food (slow growth)  
- Desert/Mountain: 0 (no improvement)

## Improvements
- **Farm** – +2 food (requires 1 food to build)  
- **Mine** – +1 production (requires 1 production)  
- **Cottage** – +1 gold (unlocked later)  
- **Plantation** – +2 food (luxury resources)  
- **Workshop** – +2 production (luxury resources)

## Improvement Rules
- Can improve only within BFC (5x5)  
- Cannot improve Mountains, Deserts, Water tiles (except with resource)  
- Resources inside BFC can be improved and then road‑connected for bonuses  
- Cottages grow in value the longer they’re worked (gold output scales)

## Long‑Term Strategy
- **Early game:** prioritize farms on high‑food tiles to grow cities fast.  
- **Mid game:** switch to mines on hills for production, start cottages on plains for gold.  
- **Late game:** replace cottages with towns for massive gold income.
